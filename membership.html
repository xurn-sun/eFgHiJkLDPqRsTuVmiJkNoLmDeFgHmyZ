Membership

Application Process

For more than two centuries, our diverse members have joined the Illuminati with their own unique reasons and backgrounds.

We welcome individuals of all faiths, genders, and walks of life. Illuminati members are driven by a genuine desire to better themselves and assist others, motivated not by obligation but by genuine compassion. As a result, our members freely offer their support without expecting anything in return.

Membership is open to any man aged 18 and above, regardless of race or religion, who demonstrates good character and citizenship. Those who join must be willing to take a solemn loyalty oath and participate in initiation rituals.

If you meet these criteria and are interested in joining, simply fill out the form below to register your interest. Our lodge’s secretary will then reach out to you with further information.

The illuminati lodge of New York is dedicated to securing the Liberty of the Individual through the discovery, expression and celebration of their true nature while intersecting and networking with fellow members .
