Our Mission

As an organization, the Illuminati Lodge of New York is dedicated to fostering personal and social advancement. Through our lodge degrees and activities, we strive to uplift individuals to higher levels of nobility and enlightenment. Our mission includes extending compassion and assistance to those facing adversity, alleviating despair, and combating vice in all its forms. We aspire to be a significant moral force for the betterment of humanity.

Within our Fellowship, emphasis is placed on transitioning from the old ways to a new and improved life, extending hospitality to travelers, and aiding those in need.

Our objectives encompass both ethical and humanitarian aims. Ethically, we focus on individual growth and development, while our humanitarian efforts extend to local, national, and international spheres. Our unwavering loyalty to our cause and dedication to the principles of friendship, fellowship, and the pursuit of a new world order guide our actions.
